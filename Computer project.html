About Bts:

  1. BTS stands for Bangtan Sonyeondan (방탄소년단) meaning ‘Bulletproof Boy Scouts’.

  2. The South Korean boyband BTS debuted in June 2013.

  3. Fans who support BTS are called ARMY, an acronym for Adorable Representative M.C. for Youth.

  4. The Bangtan Boys trained for nearly three years before their debut in 2013.

  5. BTS has become the first K-pop group to ever receive a Grammy Award nomination.
